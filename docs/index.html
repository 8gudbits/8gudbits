About

The world of technology opened to me mainly via Minecraft. I went about exploring its mechanics, automation, and technical game-playing. Unfortunately, I lost my YouTube channel in early 2021, which led me to focus more on cybersecurity and scripting. That deeper interest resulted in Linux, automation, and building tools to solve actual problems. Now, at the age of 19, I build apps, utilities, and security tools for multiple platforms, from command-line interface (CLI) scripts to full-stack web applications, with privacy, automation, and minimalist design. I study business, while the technical journey has all been self-taught. I learn through building, experimenting, and troubleshooting. To me, tech is more than just a skill. It is about creating scalable and meaningful solutions.
